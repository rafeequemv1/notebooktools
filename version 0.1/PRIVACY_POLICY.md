# Privacy Policy — NotebookTools v0.1

**Last updated:** June 2026

NotebookTools is an independent Chrome extension that helps you send YouTube video URLs to your NotebookLM account.

## What we collect

NotebookTools v0.1 does not operate a backend server and does not collect analytics.

- The extension reads the active YouTube video URL when you choose to import.
- The extension may store lightweight preferences locally in Chrome storage on your device.
- Notebook list data is fetched from NotebookLM using your existing signed-in Google session.

## What we send

When you import a video, the extension sends the YouTube URL to NotebookLM through Google's NotebookLM web APIs, using the same browser session you already use at notebooklm.google.com.

## What we do not do

- We do not sell personal data.
- We do not receive your Google password.
- We do not upload page content beyond the YouTube URL you choose to import.

## Third-party services

YouTube and NotebookLM are operated by Google. Your use of those services is subject to Google's terms and privacy policies. NotebookTools is not affiliated with Google.
