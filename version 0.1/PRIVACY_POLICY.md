# Privacy Policy — NotebookTools v0.2.8

**Last updated:** June 2026  
**Published at:** https://notebooktools.com/privacy

NotebookTools is an independent Chrome extension that helps you import content into your Google NotebookLM account — including YouTube videos, X (Twitter) posts and articles, webpage URLs, and text you highlight on the web.

## What we collect

NotebookTools does not operate a backend server and does not collect analytics.

- The extension reads page content **only when you choose to import**.
- The extension may store lightweight preferences locally in Chrome storage on your device.
- Notebook list data is fetched from NotebookLM using your existing signed-in Google session.

## What we send

When you click import, the extension sends the relevant source data to NotebookLM through Google's NotebookLM web APIs, using the same browser session you already use at [notebooklm.google.com](https://notebooklm.google.com/). This may include YouTube URLs, webpage URLs, extracted X post or article text, and text you selected on a page.

## What we do not do

- We do not sell personal data.
- We do not receive your Google password.
- We do not upload content unless you explicitly trigger an import.
- We do not log browsing history or track user activity on NotebookTools servers.
- We do not use remote code.

## Third-party services

YouTube, X, and NotebookLM are operated by their respective providers. NotebookTools is not affiliated with Google or any of these platforms.
