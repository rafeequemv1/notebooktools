FAQ

Common questions

What is NotebookTools?

NotebookTools is a free Chrome extension that sends YouTube videos, ChatGPT and Gemini chats, X posts, webpages, and highlighted text directly into Google NotebookLM — using your existing Google sign-in.

Is NotebookTools free?

Yes. Every feature is free with no paywall, subscription, or premium tier.

How do I import a YouTube video to NotebookLM?

Install NotebookTools in Chrome, open NotebookLM once to sign in, visit any YouTube watch page, click the NotebookLM import button, and choose a notebook. The video is added as a source in one click.

Does NotebookTools work with ChatGPT and Gemini?

Yes. NotebookTools adds an in-page button at the top of ChatGPT and Gemini conversations. It extracts the full chat and imports it as text into NotebookLM.

Does NotebookTools collect my data?

No backend server and no analytics. Imports go directly from your browser to NotebookLM using your session. See our privacy policy.
