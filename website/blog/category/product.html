About NotebookTools

NotebookTools is a free Chrome extension built for people who use Google NotebookLM as a research hub. It adds import buttons on YouTube watch pages, X posts, and any webpage — plus a right-click action to send highlighted text straight into a notebook. No new account, no backend servers, and no paywall. These posts explain what the extension does, who it is for, and how it fits alongside your existing NotebookLM workflow. Install from the Chrome Web Store and start importing in under two minutes.
